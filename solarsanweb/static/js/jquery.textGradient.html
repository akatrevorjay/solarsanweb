Summary

BeeLine Reader is an exciting new technology that helps people read faster on computers, smartphones, and tablets.BeeLine Reader applies a color gradient to text that helps reduce “line transition errors” (accidentally skipping a line or re-reading the same line twice). The color gradient generated by BeeLine Reader helps guide the reader’s eyes from the end of one line to the beginning of the next, which significantly reduces the chance of accidentally skipping or repeating lines.

How does it help?

By making line transitions easier and more natural, BeeLine Reader allows the reader to transition more quickly from line to line.
This increases reading speed, particularly on mobile devices that have small screens and short lines.

A study designed and carried out by a Stanford graduate student of Communications showed an average reading speed increase of over 10 percent for first time users of BeeLine Reader. Many seasoned users experience speed increases of 25 to 30 percent!

Think of how much time you spend reading (email, news, textbooks, work) each day. If you read just two hours each day, BeeLine Reader could save you hours every week!
